Privacy Policy – Chronio Time Tracker

Chronio Time Tracker is a time tracking and day planning app that respects your privacy.

No account with the developer is required to use the app.
No advertising is included.
No ad tracking or behavioral analytics for marketing purposes are performed by the app.

Data you enter or that is generated on your device remains primarily on-device: personal tasks and time tracking (SwiftData), preferences and settings (local storage on the device). Calendar access (EventKit) is used to display your agenda and compute work-related indicators; that data is processed on the device.

If you configure **Jira** integration, your credentials (including the API token) are **stored only on your device**; communication occurs **directly between the app and Atlassian servers** — the developer does not operate an intermediary server and does not receive this information.
